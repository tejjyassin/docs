# Posts

Posts are related to the news "ActualitÃ©s" section on the website. They are used to create/edit and delete articles. Once created, they are displayed on the home page and the actualites page. 

## Adding a new post

To add a new post, you need to go to the "Posts" section on the left menu. Then, click on "Add New Post" on the top left corner.

![Add new post](/screenshots/posts.png)*Posts*

You will get a new page with a title, content editor and a sidebar that contain some extra information. 

![Add new post](/screenshots/add-new-post.png)*Posts->Add New Post*

1. **Title**: The title of the article. It will be displayed on the home page and the actualites page.
2. **Content**: The content of the article. You can use the editor to add images, videos, links, etc.
3. **Sidebar**: on your right side, you will find the sidebar that contains some extra information about the post. below are some of the important fields:
    1. **Publish**: The status of the article. You can either publish it or save it as a draft.
    3. **Featured Image**: The image that will be displayed on the home page and the actualites page.
    4. **Categories**: The category of the article. It will be displayed on the home page and the actualites page.

## Updating a post

Modifying an existing post closely mirrors the process of creating a new one. Just click on the post you wish to update, make the necessary changes, and don't forget to save your edits by clicking the "Update" button positioned at the top-right corner.

## Deleting a post

To delete a post, you need to go to the "Posts" section on the left menu. Then, click on the post you wish to delete. Finally, click on the "Move to Trash" button on the top right corner. 

If you did this by mistake, you can go to the "Trash" section on the top menu on the posts page and restore the post.
