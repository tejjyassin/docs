## Bloc tarification avec details

<Row>
  <Col>

   Tarification bloc is where we specify the price and advantages of our proposed service, we used it on the honoraire page. 

    ### Available settings

<Properties>
  <Property name="Titre" type="texte">
    The title of the section
  </Property>

   <Property name="Description" type="texte">
    The description of the section
  </Property>

   <Property name="Features title" type="groupe">
    Title inside the pricing box
  </Property>

  <Property name="Features" type="texte">
    A list of advantages to be included on the box, please make sure to add a total of 4 to 6 advantages.
  </Property>

  <Property name="Pricing title" type="texte">
    The title you want add next to pricing
  </Property>

  <Property name="Pricing" type="texte">
    The exact pricing of the service
  </Property>

  <Property name="Currency" type="texte">
    Think of it as a subtitle for your pricing, it can be anything.
  </Property>

  <Property name="Pricing action" type="groupe">
    Include two items, the button text and the link of the button
  </Property>

  <Property name="Pricing disclaimer" type="texte">
    Used if you want to include a disclaimer a the end of the pricing box
  </Property>

</Properties>

  </Col>
